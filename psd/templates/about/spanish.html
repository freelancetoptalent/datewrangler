About the Spanish Language Option with Poly Speed Dating

{% endblock %} {% block content %}

It is very important to us that we promote inclusive events and based on that we wanted to attempt to better accommodate those who speak Spanish as a primary language (although we recognize that this event is very English-language intensive).

We therefore have a "Spanish" checkbox so we can attempt to preferentially match those who can speak Spanish to people where such facility could improve their speed dating experience.

If you check the box, we will prioritize matches with other Spanish-language speakers that match on the specifics of your profile, but you will still be matched with people who did not check the box as long as they fit your other preferences.

If you do not check the box, it will have no effect on your matching whether potential matches did check the box or not.
