Unlike with gender and age, we don't guarantee that your matches will fit what you ask for in this section, and no matter what answers you give in this section, you will get the same number of dates.

So what do we use these questions for? We try to nudge people who might be slightly better matches for one another together. For example, if you say you are looking to meet geeky people, you will probably have more geeky dates than random chance would produce, but you will still also be matched with non-geeky people.
