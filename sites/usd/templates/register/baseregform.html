What is speed dating?
Speed Dating is a very quick way to meet a lot of people. Over the course of the evening you will go on several short dates with several different people. After each date, you will mark down a "yes" or "no" on a sheet of paper. At the end of the evening, we'll find all the pairs where both parties said "yes" and swap email addresses for those parties. If you say "yes" to someone, you aren't necessarily agreeing to go on a 'real' date or engage in any particular activity with them. But hopefully when matches happen, one of you will email the other and say, "Hey, we matched! Want to continue that conversation?"
